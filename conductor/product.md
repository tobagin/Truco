# Initial Concept
A digital implementation of the Truco card game for Linux using Vala, GTK4, and Libadwaita.

# Product Guide: Truco

## Vision
To create the definitive digital Truco experience for the Linux desktop, preserving the rich cultural heritage of the game while providing a modern, polished, and accessible application for players of all skill levels.

## Target Audience
- **Casual Players:** Individuals looking for a quick, enjoyable game on their Linux desktop.
- **Competitive Practitioners:** Players seeking to sharpen their skills against challenging AI opponents.
- **Cultural Enthusiasts:** Fans of traditional card games interested in exploring and playing various regional rule sets (Argentine, Brazilian, Spanish, etc.).

## Key Features
- **Polished Native Experience:** Built with GTK4 and Libadwaita, ensuring a seamless and beautiful integration with the GNOME desktop environment.
- **Regional Rule Mastery:** Faithful implementation of diverse regional variations, including Truco Argentino, Paulista, Mineiro, and Spanish variations.
- **Educational Support:** An interactive tutorial and comprehensive help documentation to guide new players and explain regional nuances.
- **Immersive Gameplay:** High-quality sound effects, customizable avatars, and a themed game area that captures the spirit of a real card table.
- **Challenging AI:** Robust single-player AI capable of providing a competitive and engaging experience.

## Primary Goals
1. **Preserve and Promote Culture:** Act as a digital repository and promotion tool for the various regional forms of Truco.
2. **Deliver Robust Single-Player:** Ensure the game is fun and challenging even without human opponents.
3. **Excellence in GNOME Development:** Demonstrate high-quality application development using the Vala and GTK ecosystem.

## User Experience Direction
The application follows a **hybrid UX approach**. It utilizes standard Libadwaita components for window management, menus, and dialogs to ensure system consistency, while featuring a custom-themed, high-contrast game area to provide an immersive and clear playing environment.
